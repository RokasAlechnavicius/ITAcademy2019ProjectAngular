Summer good deeds project!
Volunteers were interested in who most needed their help in their communities, talked with elders, representatives of various care organizations, worked in teams, where they decided to contribute to the change. Many of us, together, can change the environment and society. That's why we invite you to participate!
Who?
Participants are invited to do good jobs together - to create volunteer teams. The captain has to register the team - others join the specific job independently.
How?
  • You can choose activities that seem meaningful and important to you. Let's go to the nearest communities, our native cities, let's talk to nearby organizations, neighborhoods, hard-working families.
  • You can also get ideas for good job from the list provided. Click the "Register" button next to the suggested ideas and the selected jobs will be automatically assigned to you.
When?
The time when volunteers can do the jobs can be determined by the team leader or volunteer who created the job description. As a participant, you could choose the time, which is convenient for him.
